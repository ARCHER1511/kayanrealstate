About the Company

Kafa’a Real Estate Appraisal is one of the first companies officially accredited by the Saudi Authority for Accredited Valuers. It is a licensed professional firm by the Ministry of Commerce and classified by the Authority for property appraisal. The company operates across all regions of the Kingdom.

Management

The company is managed by a qualified Saudi team holding university degrees in fields directly related to real estate appraisal. All team members are fellows of the Saudi Authority for Accredited Valuers and possess practical experience in property valuation. The company has extensive experience in appraising properties for various government and private entities.
